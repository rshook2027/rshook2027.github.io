Erika Shook
Petrarchan Sonnet

"Wistful Melodies"
In verses sung, a tale we can’t undo
Leaving me ensnared in a silent space
Easier to hate than miss, a cold embrace
Yet, I’m reluctant to feel this way for you

Already through with thoughts, a heavy view
Tired of thinking, weariness to trace
Keeping you close, in a loveless chase
Honesty would soften this love’s adieu

‘Cause keeping you close, it should be serene
If your silent plea was filled with the truth
Played with my pride, a complicated scene
Making me believe in a false decree
But forcing you to stay feels like youth
This is the last suit, set my heart free


Shakespearean Sonnet

"Weekend Echoes"
In weekend’s outcry, distant and near
Open doors, choices lingering wide
Thrown into depths, waves of doubt appear
Barely afloat, in the tempest’s tide
Begging for notice, a silent plea
Time eludes, a dance in the shadows
Affection withheld, a mystery
Yet, I offer mine in quiet meadows

Curiosity’s yearning, a time of defiance
Out of bounds, out of stock
Seeking peace of mind, a need of silence
In the search for solace, a tranquil walk

Whispers emerge over the weekend recounted
In the stillness of resolution, a story reiterated


Gesu Villamil
Petrarchan Sonnet

"SPACE: The Premise"
Doth thou remember that night’s waning moon?—
Bare were the bruises, and our joyous ode
Dwelling in these embalmed chapters we wrote
The moonlight, benign, as we mocked silver spoons,
Withered like manors turned to ashy dunes
Our love, our laughter, and what stars bestowed—
Oh my dear maiden, why hath fate forebode? “Distance bore fondness”, the twain did oppugn

Must thou oppose the heavens’ refute
Tainted by palettes of shaded disdain
As red strings encompassed thine coffin beaut’
I reserved my sight for thou to obtain
For celestine bodies had pleaded “depart!”
In astronomy, we're two worlds apart.


Shakespearean Sonnet

"The Melancholic Marathon"
Evident were the lies thee had conceit—
Our existence, condemned, as though stars implored
Yet why must I submit to cold deceit—
If, an heirloom, is how thee take me for?
“Captive” was a title I upheld for thus—
Once, I was free of entangled knots
Torn were our precious tales collecting dust,
As thee dare spoke of thine lady in locks

Gone were the nights I served as a hound
Dear, here I lie from the stab of a bow
I shan’t fear the strings that were deemed to “bound”
As hereonforth, I fly free as a crow

The night love hath lost as I grew immune
Believed to have once been a trick of the moon


Gieul Montefalco
Petrarchan Sonnet

"Is hope enough to turn lies into truths?"
In this world where only lies are safe to show,
An everyday cycle, telling pretty lies that pleases everybody to the core,
Being considerate to not hurt anybody anymore.
But is it really worth it though?

Lies can be told even by those whose knowledge of love is low.
Like people who haven't loved, or been loved by anybody before.
Saying, “I love you!” Even when there’s really no love in store.
Lying to hide the ugly truths no one ever wanted to know.

Just one “I love you!” and now everyone is captivated,
Can’t take my eyes off you, even knowing everything is untrue;
Everything you do seems acted but perfected.
Even when it all is a lie, how did you stay calm and collected?
Do you wish for that lie to come true?
Or do you believe that acting is the only way to ever be accepted?


Shakespearean Sonnet

"Why am I stuck in this dream if mine?"
I am stuck in this panorama of time, frozen.
Reliving the times that i thought destiny bounded us together,
Still had not moved on, still broken,
Looking back at moments I wanted to be in forever.

As I look at the clear blue sky, memories of us two flash by,
Memories I would like to return to, times before everything turned to rubble.
Along with my dream that I know I will never reach, no matter how hard I try,
Like the future that got out of my grasps, and slipped away as I watched it crumble.

I know, I know, I’m filled with loneliness, lacking your affection.
With you being colder than before, As you left me here as time passes through.
Your change has been very apparent in both your words and actions,
Now I'm looking back wondering what it was I'm still holding onto.

I will recreate the bright scene i think of,
I'm moving with my life, going to new horizons, and now I take off.
