History Of Biofysica

Liberale Biofysica had first sprouted its roots on December 1, 2023 in a small garden in Hindikonaalam City. Unlike any mere tree, it had grown and survived without any TLC. The young founders, Shooketh, Montefalcon, and Villamillionaire, were a group of friends that had grown fond of the tree's peculiar aura. It was extraordinarily large, though having a very hollow trunk, it still managed to stand tall and remain in position.

The three founders had stopped by the tree day by day, then suddenly, they thought that it'd be a great idea to run a school in the tree. One of the three friends, Montefalcon, had hesitated at first, thinking that the idea was absurd and impossible. Though the other two had managed to convince him by telling him that "According to the bippitty boppitty boo theory of G.M. Fairy, "Every inaninimate object has had a slim chance at life, and only magic can make it a reality."

Years later, the tree grew and grew, rooms of strong oak wood and infinite amounts of papers were made enough to supply a hundred students. Hundreds turned into thousands, hundred-thousands, and the now, three-quntillion student enrollees! All three founders had sacrificed their lives for the tree, and had decided to offer their souls to bless the tree with everlasting knowledge, joy, and hope. Also known as the three sacred seeds which students now pledge allegiance to.
