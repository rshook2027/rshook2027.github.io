BI-YOW

Do you have a passion in biology? or a passion in arts? Well, if you've said yes to either then our doors are WIDE OPEN here at BI-YOW! BI-YOW! is a club that combines the arts and the sciences by letting Members introduce/implement a wide variety of activities that cover BIOLOGY AS A WHOLE. Whether it may be a theatre play taking a playful turn on the animal kingdom, or a collaborative student portfolio with hundreds of floral displays...let your creative juices FLOW here at BI-YOW! Our name "BI-YOW!" was derived from our first ever club founders' introductory project where they've rapped their way through discussing anatomy!!! Which then lead to the implementation of the beloved biology club we all know of today.

XPORTS
